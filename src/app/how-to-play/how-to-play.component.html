How to play

Guess the Operator.

The operator is selected randomly every time the page is loaded from all the available operators of the Ubisoft's game “Tom Clancy's: Rainbow Six Siege”. There are currently 66 operators, the last added being Solis. It changes every time you refresh the page.

After each guess, the color of the tiles will change to show how close your guess was to the Operator.

Green indicates the property is an exact match.

Orange indicates partial match.

Red indicates there is no match.

Arrows indicates the answer property is above or below your guess.

Properties

Here is the details of each of the properties columns:

Gender: Male, Female or Other.

Health: One (100), Two (110) or Three (125).

Role: Intel, Support, Map control, Anti-gadget, etc...

Affiliation: S.A.S., SWAT, GIGN, Spetsnaz, etc...

Squad: Redhammer, Viperstrike, Wolfguard, etc...

Release Season: Launch, Black Ice, Dust Line, Skull Rain, etc...

Example

Consider the correct answer is Amaru.

If you enter Ash, these properties will appear:

Female
  • Breach,
  • Front line
  • SWAT
  • Launch

Gender: It is an exact match, they are both female.

Health: It is not a match since Amaru has 2 health.

Role: It is partially correct since Amaru is also Front line, but not the others.

Affiliation: It is not correct since Amaru is not from the SWAT.

Squad: It is correct, they are both Redhammers.

Release Season: Amaru's release season is after Launch.
